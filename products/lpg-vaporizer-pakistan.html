How It Works

How an LPG Vaporizer Works

An LPG vaporizer converts liquid-phase LPG from storage tanks into vapor phase for use in industrial burners, boilers, and processes. This is necessary when natural vaporization from the tank surface is insufficient to meet your demand — especially in cold weather or with large consumption rates.

The AAA Gas Engineering vaporizer uses an electric water-bath heating system: an immersion heater warms a water bath, which transfers heat to a stainless steel coil carrying liquid LPG. The LPG vaporizes inside the coil and exits as gas at controlled pressure. A float valve and level switch prevent liquid LPG from flowing downstream.

The system is fully automatic with interlocks for pressure, temperature, and liquid level — ensuring safe, consistent operation around the clock.
