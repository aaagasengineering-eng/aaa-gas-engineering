About Us

Engineering Trust
Since 1995

AAA Gas Engineering is a proud branch of Major Enterprises, one of Pakistan's most trusted names in LPG equipment manufacturing. Based in Gujranwala, we have been delivering high-quality, safety-compliant LPG solutions to industries across Pakistan for over three decades.

Our products are engineered to international safety standards and approved by OGRA, ISO, PEC, and the Chamber of Commerce — ensuring every piece of equipment leaving our facility meets the highest benchmarks of quality and safety.
