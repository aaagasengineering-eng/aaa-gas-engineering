Our Story

A Branch of Major
Enterprises

AAA Gas Engineering is a proud branch of Major Enterprises, one of Pakistan's most established names in LPG equipment. Founded in 1995 in Gujranwala, Punjab, we have grown from a local manufacturing unit into one of Pakistan's leading LPG equipment manufacturers.

Over 30 years, we have supplied thousands of industrial and commercial clients across Pakistan — from textile mills and hospitals in Punjab to food processing plants in Karachi and Lahore.

Our manufacturing facility operates under ISO 9001 quality standards. We are OGRA approved, PEC registered, and members of the Chamber of Commerce.

Our Journey

30 Years of Growth

1995
Foundation
Major Enterprises established in Gujranwala. Started manufacturing LPG cylinder valves for the local market.
2000
Product Expansion
Expanded to LPG regulators and pressure control equipment. Customer base grew across Punjab.
2005
ISO Certification
Achieved ISO 9001 quality management certification. Upgraded manufacturing with precision CNC equipment.
2010
OGRA Approval
Received official OGRA approval for all LPG equipment. Began supplying to large industrial clients including textile factories and hospitals.
2015
LPG Vaporizer Manufacturing
Launched AAA-brand LPG vaporizer line. Became one of the first Pakistani manufacturers to produce water-bath vaporizers domestically.
2020
AAA Gas Engineering Brand
Established AAA Gas Engineering as a dedicated brand. Launched digital presence and online ordering.
2025+
International Expansion
Expanding into Middle East and Africa. Serving 5000+ clients across all of Pakistan.
